About SKT Group

Leading the market through superior customer satisfaction since 1988

Who We Are

SKT Group is a premier 360° hospitality consultancy based in Kolkata, India. With over 36 years of industry experience, we have established ourselves as trusted partners for hotels, restaurants, and hospitality businesses across the country.

Our comprehensive suite of services encompasses hotel management, financial advisory, interior design, manpower solutions, training and education, digital marketing, event management, and hotel equipment solutions.

We believe in leading the market through superior customer satisfaction and sustaining long-term, profitable relationships with our clients while adding tangible value to their businesses.
